Nabla offers 15 note templates, each tuned for a specific clinical context. Picking the right one upfront saves you from over-customizing later.

## By specialty

| You're documenting | Start with | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Primary care, general medicine | `GENERIC_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` or `GENERIC_SOAP` | Broad coverage, the most flexible templates. |
| Pediatric well-child visits | `WCC_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` or `WCC_SOAP` | Sections tailored for growth, milestones, and anticipatory guidance. |
| Emergency department | `EMERGENCY_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` or `EMERGENCY_SOAP` | Sections for triage, disposition, and procedures. |
| Cardiology | `CARDIOLOGY_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` | Cardiovascular history and risk factors front-and-center. |
| Psychiatry | `PSYCHIATRY_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` | Mental status exam and risk assessment sections. |
| Psychology | `PSYCHOLOGY_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` | History of present complaint and intervention plan. |
| Dietetics / nutrition | `DIET_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS` | Food habits, lifestyle, objectives and advice. |

## SOAP vs multi-section

Each specialty (or generic) typically comes in two flavours:

- **`*_SOAP`** — strict four-block layout (Subjective / Objective / Assessment / Plan). Good when downstream EHR fields are SOAP-shaped.
- **`*_MULTIPLE_SECTIONS`** — richer layout with separate sections like Chief Complaint, HPI, Past Medical History, Allergies, Vitals, A&P, etc. Good when you want more structure than SOAP imposes.

If your downstream EHR uses problem-list-driven A&P, also pass `note_sections_customization` with `split_by_problem: true` on the relevant section. See [Section customization](/core-api/note-templates/customization).

## Locale support

Note generation locales are limited to `ENGLISH_US`, `ENGLISH_UK`, and `FRENCH_FR`. Not every template is available in every locale — check the **Remarks** column in the [Templates reference](/core-api/note-templates/sections) before settling on a pair.

## Switching templates later

Templates are picked per request — there's no global "template setting" on your organization. You can ship multiple templates from the same app and let providers pick. For User API integrations, you can also persist a default per user via `PATCH /note-settings`.
