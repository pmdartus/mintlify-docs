## What is Nabla Connect?

Nabla Connect is the simplest way to embed Nabla's clinical AI capabilities into your product. Where the [Core API](/core-api/introduction) gives you direct programmatic access to transcription, note generation, and structured data, Connect provides a higher-level integration surface designed to ship faster.
